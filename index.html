About Institution


A renowned institution of higher learning, Vignan's Institute of Information Technology has its roots in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh since 2002. Vignan's Institute of Information Technology offers 22 diverse courses for Degree in Full Time mode. Vignan's Institute of Information Technology offers a range of 11 UG courses. These courses are aimed to provide students with the necessary skills and knowledge in their field of interest.
